## Business Context

### The Challenge

AI adoption is accelerating across enterprises, but governance frameworks struggle to keep pace:

- **Shadow AI**: Uncontrolled AI usage creates security and compliance risks
- **Cost Explosion**: Unchecked AI spend with no visibility or accountability
- **Compliance Gaps**: Existing frameworks don't address AI-specific requirements
- **Operational Overhead**: Manual governance processes can't scale

### The Solution

Citadel Governance Hub provides a **unified governance layer** that sits alongside your existing Azure infrastructure:
