## What is Citadel Governance Hub?

Citadel Governance Hub is a **comprehensive AI governance solution** that enables enterprises to deploy, manage, and secure AI workloads at scale. Built on Microsoft Azure, it provides centralized policy enforcement, cost control, and compliance monitoring across all AI services—from Azure OpenAI to AI Foundry to custom models.

**In one sentence**: Citadel Governance Hub turns AI governance from a cost center into a competitive advantage by enabling secure, scalable, and cost-effective AI adoption.

## The Business Challenge

### AI Adoption is Accelerating—Governance is Lagging

Organizations across every industry are racing to adopt AI for competitive advantage. However, this rapid adoption creates significant business risks:

<Callout type="warning">
**Shadow AI Risk**

Without centralized governance, teams deploy AI services independently, creating:
- **Security vulnerabilities**: Sensitive data exposed to unmanaged AI endpoints
- **Compliance gaps**: Unaudited AI usage violates regulatory requirements
- **Cost overruns**: Unchecked AI spend with no accountability or visibility
- **Operational chaos**: Inconsistent security practices across teams
</Callout>

### Regulatory Pressure is Increasing

AI regulations are emerging globally:
- **EU AI Act**: Risk-based classification with strict compliance requirements
- **US Executive Orders**: Federal AI governance mandates
- **Industry Regulations**: Financial services, healthcare, and government-specific AI guidelines
- **Existing Frameworks**: SOC 2, GDPR, HIPAA now include AI-specific requirements

Organizations that fail to establish AI governance now face:
- Regulatory penalties and legal exposure
- Reputational damage from AI incidents
- Competitive disadvantage from delayed AI adoption
- Higher costs from retrofitting governance later
