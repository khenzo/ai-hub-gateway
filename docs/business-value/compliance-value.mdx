### Audit Complexity with Distributed AI

<Callout type="warning">
**The Distributed AI Problem**

Without centralized governance, AI usage becomes invisible to compliance teams:

- **Shadow AI**: Teams deploy AI services outside approved channels
- **Data Sprawl**: Sensitive data flows to unmanaged AI endpoints
- **Model Drift**: Production models evolve without compliance review
- **Evidence Gaps**: No audit trail for AI decision-making
- **Access Chaos**: Inconsistent identity and access controls
</Callout>
