## Overview

This page maps every requirement from the EY AI Agent Development Framework to its corresponding Citadel component or architecture layer. Use the enforcement status column to identify what Citadel covers, what requires supplemental process, and where to look for additional tooling.

### Summary

<Info>
  **Enforced**: Citadel natively implements 14 of 27 criteria through Azure infrastructure, APIM policies, or the 4-layer architecture — no additional tooling required.
</Info>

<Warning>
  **Partial**: 10 criteria require Citadel plus an EY process step (AIRA submission, AI QRM form, service-line approval) or a supplemental tool for full coverage. The Attention Points section details what to add.
</Warning>

<Note>
  **Attention Points**: 3 criteria fall outside Citadel's scope: NVIDIA stack, EY-proprietary tooling, and No-code/Low-code platforms. These are by design — Citadel targets Azure-native pro-code deployments.
</Note>
