## Key Terminology

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Citadel">
    The governance hub that provides centralized control and visibility over AI infrastructure deployed across your organization.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Landing Zone">
    A pre-configured, secure environment for deploying AI workloads, including network isolation, identity management, and AI service integration.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Access Contract">
    A policy document defining the terms under which an application can consume AI services, including rate limits, budgets, and content filters.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Token">
    The billing unit for AI model consumption, typically representing a word or part of a word. Both input and output tokens are counted.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Hub and Spoke">
    A network topology where the Citadel hub contains shared services and landing zone spokes connect to the hub for governance and shared resources.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
