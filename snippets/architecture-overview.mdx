## Architecture Overview

The AI Landing Zone implements the **Citadel Architecture** — a comprehensive framework for deploying and governing enterprise AI infrastructure at scale. The architecture follows a 4-layer model:

### The 4-Layer Model

<Steps>
  <Step title="Layer 1: Foundation">
    Core infrastructure including networking, identity (Azure AD), Key Vault, and Log Analytics.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Layer 2: AI Services">
    Managed AI capabilities: Azure OpenAI Service, AI Search, and ML Workspaces.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Layer 3: Governance">
    Policy enforcement, access contracts, rate limiting, and cost controls.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Layer 4: Applications">
    AI-powered solutions consuming governed services through the Citadel hub.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Hub and Spoke Topology

The Citadel architecture uses a hub-and-spoke network model where the **Citadel Hub** contains shared services (API Management, monitoring, governance) and **Landing Zone Spokes** contain AI workloads that connect to the hub.
